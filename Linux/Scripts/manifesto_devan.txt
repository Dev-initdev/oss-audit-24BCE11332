========================================
 OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO
 Written by: devan
 Date: 31 March 2026
========================================

I believe in software that is built to be shared.

Every day I rely on firefox, a tool that someone
built and gave away freely so that others could
stand on their work rather than reinvent it.
That act, choosing to share instead of hoard,
is what I understand as Liberty.

I commit to carrying that forward. One day I want
to build storage Application and release it as open source,
so that someone I will never meet can use it,
improve it, and pass it on.

Open source is not just a license. It is a
decision about what kind of world we want to
live in: one where knowledge belongs to everyone,
or one where it is locked behind profit.

I choose open.

-- devan, 31 March 2026
